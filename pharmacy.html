Our Medications

Paracetamol

Paracetamol is a common painkiller used to treat aches and pain. It can also be used to reduce a high temperature.

  • Relieves mild to moderate pain
  • Reduces fever
  • Available in tablet, liquid, and syrup forms

Ibuprofen

Ibuprofen is a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) used for treating pain, fever, and inflammation.

  • Effective for muscle pain and arthritis
  • Helps reduce fever
  • Available in tablet, gel, and cream forms

Amoxicillin

Amoxicillin is an antibiotic used to treat a variety of bacterial infections.

  • Treats bacterial infections
  • Commonly prescribed for respiratory tract infections
  • Available in capsule, tablet, and liquid forms

Lisinopril

Lisinopril is an angiotensin-converting enzyme (ACE) inhibitor used to treat high blood pressure and heart failure.

  • Helps lower blood pressure
  • Reduces the risk of stroke and heart attack
  • Available in tablet form

Metformin

Metformin is used to treat type 2 diabetes by helping control blood sugar levels.

  • Improves blood sugar control
  • Reduces the risk of heart disease in diabetics
  • Available in tablet form

Atorvastatin

Atorvastatin is a statin used to lower cholesterol and reduce the risk of heart disease.

  • Lowers bad cholesterol (LDL)
  • Increases good cholesterol (HDL)
  • Available in tablet form
